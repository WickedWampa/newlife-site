Services

We’re here to help men build recovery in a structured residential setting.

New Life Sober Living serves men recovering from drug and alcohol addiction and provides a sober living environment that supports stability before reintegration back into society.

Structured sober living

A residential environment built around routine, responsibility, and a clear commitment to sobriety.

AA and NA participation

Residents are required to participate in programs that support the philosophy of AA and NA.

Guidance and support

Residents can also be connected with recovery coaches and therapists when needed.

What we offer

Comfortable living with practical support.

  • Each room includes cable TV and AC
  • Electricity, heat, and water included
  • Off-street parking
  • In-house washer and dryer

Recovery focus

Support that goes beyond just a place to stay.

Throughout their stay, residents are encouraged to build recovery through participation, accountability, trauma-informed growth, and personal development.

The goal is to provide not just housing, but a stronger foundation for lasting sobriety.
