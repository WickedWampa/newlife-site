About us

A supportive, structured environment for recovery.

At New Life Sober Living, recovery is not something you have to face alone. Residents are encouraged to participate in programs that support the philosophy of Alcoholics Anonymous and Narcotics Anonymous while building daily habits that promote sobriety and personal progress.

Safe sober housing

A stable place to live where men can focus on recovery, structure, and rebuilding their lives.

Peer support

A brotherhood-centered environment where residents work together to create a culture of sobriety.

Recovery resources

Support may include connections to recovery coaches, therapists, psychiatrists, career opportunities, budgeting, and credit-building resources.

Our mission

Helping men build lasting sobriety and a more stable future.

New Life Sober Living exists to provide men in recovery with a safe, nurturing, and recovery-focused home. The aim is to foster lasting sobriety through structure, participation, accountability, and access to meaningful support.

Residents are encouraged to incorporate recovery principles into daily life, build healthier habits, and take practical steps toward long-term success.

“Don’t just settle. Reprogram your mind and body. Transform your life.”

Contact New Life Sober Living to learn more about availability, expectations, and the next step in your recovery journey.

Meet our owner

Davin Freitas

Davin Freitas brings personal experience to the mission of New Life Sober Living. After his own long battle with addiction, he committed himself to helping other men find recovery, structure, and the resources needed to succeed.

Today, he operates New Life Sober Living in North Attleboro to help men who are ready to build a better life in sobriety.
